Why Professional Leak Detection?

Professional pool screen leak detection specialists have advanced equipment and expertise to locate leaks quickly and accurately. They can identify hidden leaks that cause water damage and provide lasting repair solutions.

Benefits of Fast Leak Repair

Quick leak repair prevents water damage, protects your pool cage structure, prevents mold growth, and saves money on water bills. Professional repairs ensure leaks don't return.

Signs of Pool Screen Leaks

Look for water stains on screen panels, dripping water during rain, pooling water around the base, sagging screens, rust on aluminum frames, or mold growth. Address leaks immediately to prevent structural damage.
